About
Hello! Thanks for visiting :)

I am a graduate student in Communication Design living and studying in New York. I primarily work in user interface and graphic design, but have a personal interest in illustration and Asian studies. I was born in Beijing, China, but also lived in Australia and Los Angeles. I moved to the United States when I was in 7th grade, straddling the space between first generation and second generation Chinese American immigrants, being neither yet relating to both. I aim to use this unique position to explore my understanding of the East and the West, and of the changes in culture and communication that occur when items are brought from one to the other.
