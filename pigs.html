About
For this project, I began by choosing the mad libs text, which was a variation of the classic childhood story of the Three Little Pigs. The random words I filled in, such as kitten, smiley, disco balls, shoes, sweet and bras, made the story sweet and girly. To complement these words, I used an image treatment that was pink and plastic-y, in the style of the early 2000s when Paris Hilton was at her peak and we all wore Juicy Couture sweatpants. This style is girly, but not quiet or submissive. Instead, it is loud, powerful, and rebellious, and shows a kind of extreme femininity that is become popular again as a tool for contemporary feminist protest.
