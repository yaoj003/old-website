About
Working under the creative director Olivia Bransbourg, I developed the branding for a new perfume house called Sous Le Manteau, or SLM. SLM is inspired by aphrodisiac potions from the Middle Ages, which have been re-contextualized into a modern day love story focusing on enchantment and seduction. I helped to create the logo and brand identity, expressing these in a website, as well as designing bottles and packaging for its line of fragrances, including a leaflet and ceramic pendant included inside.
